Rules

Objectives:

Get closer to 21 than your opponent

If you surpass 21, you lose!

How to play:

Take a card from the deck

Keep taking cards until you get close enough to 21

When you want to stop taking cards, fold

Card Values:

Number: Value of its own number

A: Can either have a value of 1 or 11

J,Q,K: Has a value of 10
